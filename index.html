About this Library Management System

This Library Management System helps the school library organize books, manage users, and make borrowing faster and easier.

Objectives

  • Provide a simple way to search and browse the book catalog
  • Support borrowing and returning with clear availability status
  • Keep accurate borrowing history for students
  • Allow admins to manage books and user accounts
